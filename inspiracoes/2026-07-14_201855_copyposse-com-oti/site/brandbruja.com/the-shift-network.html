The Shift Network

Creative Director (2020–2022)

As Creative Director, I led the end-to-end transformation of The Shift Network’s global brand ecosystem during a major growth phase. I was brought in to modernize the visual identity, unify creative across departments, and elevate the experience of a $20M+ education platform serving millions of students worldwide.


My Role

I partnered closely with leadership to drive a full creative evolution across:

Brand identity + visual systems
Website + product experience
Marketing campaigns + funnels
Summit and event branding
Faculty / talent presentations
Digital assets, video, and storytelling

This was not a “make things pretty” role  - this was creative strategy, systems design, and narrative direction across the entire company.
